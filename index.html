About BBO.HK â British Biology Olympiad Information Portal

The British Biology Olympiad (BBO) is the UK's longest-running and most influential secondary school biology competition, established in 1995 and organized by the Royal Society of Biology (RSB). Nearly 10,000 UK high school students participate annually, with top performers selected to represent the UK at the International Biology Olympiad (IBO).

The BBO exam is a 90-minute individual written test covering seven areas: Cell Biology (20%), Animal Anatomy & Physiology (25%), Plant Anatomy & Physiology (15%), Genetics & Evolution (20%), Ecology (10%), Ethology (5%), and Biosystematics (5%). Awards: Gold (~top 5%), Silver (~top 15%), Bronze (~top 30%), Highly Commended (~top 45%), Commended (~top 60%). 2024 Gold cutoff: 113.20 points.

BBO achievements significantly strengthen UCAS applications for medicine, biology, and STEM at Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College London, and other top UK universities.

BBO.HK is operated by Aston Education, a Hong Kong-based education consultancy established in 2000. Aston is a UCAS registered centre and International Olympiad official exam centre, partnering with 680+ UK and Australian institutions.

Aston Education provides comprehensive BBO support:

  • Registration guidance and process support
  • One-on-one academic tutoring for BBO
  • Past paper analysis and mock exams
  • A-Level Biology course reinforcement
  • UCAS personal statement coaching
  • Full UK university application services
